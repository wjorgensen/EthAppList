# EthAppList.com

EthAppList aims to solve the problem that it's hard to find quality blockchain projects, new and old, because there is no directory of Ethereum based applications. It is a cross between ProductHunt and Wikipedia where users get to dictate, with their feedback, the products that top the charts. 

EthAppList will become the place everyone new or experienced in the Web3 world will go to find innovative products to try out whether newly launched or with a rich track record. It aims to be an entertaining website that users will scroll for fun to find new products to try out and updates on their favorite products.

Note from the creator: I hate oboarding new people into Web3 because I dont have one place I can direct them to to find out about the various Web3 products so it ends up with me giving them a big list of super varied and complex products to look into which becomes a monumental task for new users. I also hate having to find out about new products through word of mouth or happening to see it on twitter. I want to solve both these problems with one directory to find any product you can think of with all of the information about it so no more reading lengthy whitepapers or crappy CoinMarketCap about sections.
